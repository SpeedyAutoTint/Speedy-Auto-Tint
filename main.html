Certified Installation

Audio, Video, Alarms & Remote Start

Every install is performed by an MECP-certified technician — the industry's gold standard for professional automotive electronics installation.

MECP Certified

MECP Certified Technician on Staff

The Mobile Electronics Certification Program (MECP) is the automotive electronics industry's only nationally recognized certification. Our technician has passed rigorous testing covering installation theory, vehicle electrical systems, signal processing, and safety standards — so your vehicle is in qualified hands every time.
